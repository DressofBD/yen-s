Our Philosophy

Here at Wasco Energy, we are driven by passion to continuously understand your needs, and deliver the most cost effective and efficient solutions to ensure success for your projects.

Our Vision

UPHOLDING SUSTAINABLE VALUES TO DELIVER EXCELLENCE IN EVERYTHING WE DO.
While we are obligated to our many stakeholders to grow our business, we constantly strive to balance out our economic ambitions with societal and environmental obligations, so that we will prosper in an ethical manner and make a positive impact on Profits, People and Planet. By inculcating a spirit of excellence in all that we set our hands to and by maintaining an esprit de corps at all levels of our organization, we believe we still stand out as a model for responsible corporate behavior.

Our Mission

PROVIDING RELIABLE AND COMPETITIVE PRODUCTS, SERVICES AND SOLUTIONS TO THE MARKETS THAT WE SERVE, WHILE CONTINUOUSLY CREATING VALUE FOR OUR CUSTOMERS, EMPLOYEES, SHAREHOLDERS AND OTHER STAKEHOLDERS.
We are committed to follow the following sustainability priorities to achieve our mission:

  • Governance

    To comply with, all relevant legislation, regulations and codes of corporate conduct and practice.

    Strategic Engagement

    To strengthen relationships with all stakeholders to ensure the sustainable development of our business, employees, communities, business partners and customers.

    Operational Efficiency

    To ensure improvements are continuously made to our operations and our supply chain in line with the highest global standards of efficiency.

    Environmental Stewardship

    To constantly measure and efficiently manage our environmental footprint in every aspect of our operations.

    Advocacy & Social Responsibility

    To contribute in a positive and tangible manner to the markets that we serve by aligning with national and global development goals and principles.

  • Health and Safety

    To ensure our work does not compromise stakeholder health or safety and to promote the safety and health of our employees, contractors, and the communities we operate in.

    Economic Responsibility

    To ensure our organization and our shareholders garner long-term and mutually beneficial economic value from our business operations.

    Product & Service Innovation

    To continually create new products, services, and processes that elevate the customer service experience, enable us to keep our competitive standing in the market.

    People Empowerment

    To create effective, conducive and empowering workplace best practices so that our employees can be the best they can be.
