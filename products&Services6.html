Aftermarket and Services

Wasco Engineering’s service offering include field services for reciprocating compressors, Engines and motors, alternators, valves, instruments, process and associated equipment.

Our service engineers undertake various levels of ad-hoc and regular service for oil and gas and industrial customers. Service support is designed for both scheduled and unscheduled maintenance. Our highly trained field service engineers have the expertise to undertake complex service support as well as advise customers on increasing uptime and reducing maintenance costs. This approach reduces overall costs for our customers by providing maintenance tips, advising on condition based monitoring and undertaking periodic inspections.

The advantages offered to client by Wasco are:

  • Scheduled & unscheduled maintenances

  • Troubleshooting and Condition-based monitoring

  • Specialized services (commissioning & consultancy).

  • Certified Engine and compressor engineers

  • Special tools for inspection & troubleshooting

  • Onshore and Offshore certifications

  • Quick response time with Centralized Support

  • Workshops in Singapore and Dubai

  • Highly skilled and certified staff for overhauling and repair

  • Onsite overhauling (minor/major) and repair solution

  • Onsite repair solution for all ancillary equipment

Wasco Engineering offers Operations and Maintenance (O&M) services for all compressor, power generation and process equipment. These services are provided as a means of complete productions support and can be customized according to customer needs. Wasco hires and trains qualified personnel to support the O&M needs to ensure maximum uptime and production

Three tiers of support are provided to allow for flexibility as per customer needs.

  • Manpower Support: Qualified personnel with centralized control are provided for operational requirements. This helps the customers to operate all equipment under the supervision of experienced personnel with field experience supported by senior engineers from operational support team of Wasco.

  • Manpower with inventory management: Along with personnel, operational spares are maintained and replenished as per requirement. This allows additional layer of support wherein the operational maintenance of equipment is undertaken as part of O&M.

  • Comprehensive support: All operational needs are managed directly by Wasco with uptime guarantees. Qualified manpower, all spares and availability guarantees are included to provide single stop solution.

Wasco provides comprehensive aftermarket product support with supply of trained manpower and original spare parts. Once clients take delivery of equipment, we work with our partners to provide spares for preventive maintenance as well as major overhauls. Technical advice to increase the life of parts by optimising equipment efficiency is also available. Swing equipment for large high value components are also available to recue downtime and production disruption. We provide factory-authorised parts at competitive prices along with our service offering to provide a single stop solution. The parts are warranted as per OEM recommendations. Wasco can also manage the inventory on behalf of customers, ensuring significant cost reductions and efficient parts utilisation.

The advantages offered to client by Wasco are:

  • Original spare parts with warranty

  • Increased Parts efficiency

  • Extended support during unscheduled inspections

  • Proactive contract management

  • Technology improvements & upgrades

  • Logistics and inventory management

  • Quick response time with Centralized Support

  • Workshops in Singapore and Dubai

  • Repair Kits for high-value items to optimize costs

  • Dedicated Contract Manager

Wasco Engineering has a comprehensive program of retrofits, revamps and upgrades to help customers save on expensive capital equipment. Equipment which has been used comprehensively can be retrofit to replace worn out parts or upgraded based on clinet requirements.

Wasco has workshop facility in Dubai, Singapore and Batam to undertake overhauling and repair works. Wasco team of experienced engineers and field personnel determine the feasibility of undertaking overhauls; provide technical advice on the best course of action and execute the agreed plan as per OEM recommendations.

Wasco can undertake overhaul of engines and compressors to ensure life of expensive capital equipment is extended. All minor and major overhauls can be carried out as per customer requirement s in our workshop or at site. Wasco also undertakes upgrade of compression equipment to mitigate the effects of age of equipment, changed operating parameters including change in well profile. These revamp guarantee production increase at very low cost compared to investment in new equipment.

Wasco Engineering has in-house capability to rebuild compressor valves and associated equipment. Repair of high value equipment is another area of expertise that our team is proficient in.

Wasco Engineering has been servicing oil and gas industry for the last 25 years and has vast experience in the field. Installation and commissioning of equipment is one of the most critical aspects of project delivery. If carried out incorrectly, it can lead to major equipment failure, production losses and has huge cost implication. Wasco’s team of highly skilled engineers in conjunction with experienced commissioning specialist provides installation and commissioning services of the highest quality. Wasco designs its equipment to help in easy installation and quick startup to ensure project commences without the lengthy delays normally associated with commissioning.

Wasco can also provide commissioning services for third party equipment. Customers who face delays on commissioning or require third part advice due to protracted unresolved issues are provided the services of our engineers who will travel to site and assist in resolving all technical issues. They are assisted by experienced team of commissioning specialists trained on extensive array of OEM equipment.

Technical support is key to successful asset management in the oil and gas sector. Wasco has dedicated centralized technical support team to provide global coverage to our existing customer and also assist to new customers who are looking for answers to their field queries. Wasco’s dedicated team can provide quick assistance and solutions for all operations problems. We also arrange field technician to travel to the site to fix any unresolved issue.

Our aim is to build customer awareness and capabilities on the equipment to ensure high availability of the equipment and the production to maximize the profit. Wasco is committed to delivering a reliable and cost-effective solution without compromising the quality services. We have strategically set up our field services hubs to allow us to reach customer site quickly. Our unique team drawn from Engineering, Services, and Project functions allow us to provide effective and optimal performance of the customer assets.
