Leasing and Rental Boom

Wasco Engineering packages and rents reciprocating gas compression and gas generator packages. Renting compression equipment or power generator provides flexibility to maintain capital-intensive oil and gas plant. It helps customer to direct his capital, expertise, and resources to their best potential.

We maintain compression rental fleets throughout the world, as well build new packages for clients to rent. Wasco installs commissions and operates these compressors. Availability guarantees can be provided to ensure customer is assured of production without worrying about performance. Wasco provides qualified and experienced manpower to operate the compressors and maintains on-site inventory to support operational needs.

Compressor and gas generator rentals are the quick, flexible and convenient option for those who are looking to operate their plant without any major capital investment. Equipment buyout option is provided once the customers have gauged performance and have funds to invest in capital equipment.

Wasco Engineering provides various solutions to customers by offering various models of leasing arrangements like Built, Own, Operate (BOO), Built, Own, Operate, Transfer(BOOT).
Wasco has successfully installed, operated and transferred gas plants at remote locations successfully. Wasco can also provide day rate basis leasing arrangement of process modules for on-shore and off-shore applications.
