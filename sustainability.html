Our Sustainability Policy

Sustaining the health of the environment and the people while maintaining a healthy bottom line involves the integration of three key factors: social, economic, and environmental. At Wasco Energy, we take this seriously. Which is why we are constantly assessing our business model to inject sustainable innovation strategies. Addressing how we can equally accommodate all three factors to meet the needs of the present without compromising the future.

Economic

At Wasco Energy, we take pride in delivering fair, transparent and ethical business practices to ensure long-term benefits for the company and the environment, without compromising quality, financial stability and profitability.

Social

We implement and abide by the the social strategies that involve creating a safe work environment, growth opportunities and respect for the community to achieve a motivated workforce with enhanced skills.

Environment

We implement cleaner technology in business operations as we see the rise in the need for more eco-friendly products. We are constantly reviewing existing processes and opting for greener alternative to reduce our carbon footprint.
