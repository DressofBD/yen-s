Ariel Compressors

Ariel Corporation is the world's largest manufacturer of separable reciprocating gas compressors. Ariel compressors are utilized by the global energy industry to extract, process, transport, store, and distribute natural gas from the wellhead to the end-user. Ariel also provides specialized API-618 reciprocating compressors for the process industry. As the largest manufacturer of reciprocating gas compressors, Ariel sets the industry standard through expert design and manufacturing, industry-leading research and development, and unmatched customer support. Wasco Energy has been involved in distributing Ariel reciprocating compressors products and services for over 17 years and has been an Ariel Approved Distributor since 2006.

Waukesha Gas Engines

Waukesha gas engines can run under high stress conditions while still having the flexibility to use multiple fuels. Waukesha engines are designed to perform reliably in isolated, mission-critical, and demanding applications. Waukesha engines meet emissions compliance levels in diverse applications around the globe. With rich and lean burn Waukesha reciprocating engine designs, we can help you make smart choices. Wasco have been supporting Waukesha gas engine products and services for over 17 years. The Waukesha gas engine driver in our compressor package design features: Long service intervals, a maintenance-friendly engine design, low fuel consumption, prolonged life of all engine components, even when using polluted fuel gases such as landfill gas, good operational safety and availability with control and monitoring systems, a broad range of commercial, industrial and municipal uses, optimised control, monitoring and information management systems, and a patented, lean mixture combustion control to minimise exhaust gas emissions while maintaining stable engine operation.

Dresser-Rand Compressors (A Siemen Business)

Dresser-Rand High and Medium Speed Reciprocating Compressors (former API 11-P)
With a versatile compressor lineup in the market and a reputation for serving both standard applications and the most demanding jobs (high pressure, CO2, sour and acid gases, and floating applications), Dresser-Rand covers virtually every application. This class of reciprocating compressors is designed to ISO 13631, ISO 13707, or API 618 specifications. Dresser-Rand offers seven different models with horsepower (hp) ranging from 200 to 11,000 hp (149 to 8,203 kW), allowable rod loads from 15,400 to 90,000 psi, rated maximum speeds from 850 to 1,800 rpm, and pressures up to 8,000 psig. MOS, HOS and HOSS Models of compressors being the workhorse of Oil and Gas industry.

BHGE Compressors

BHGE's field proven portfolio of high-speed reciprocating (HSR) compressors deliver maximum flexibility and minimal cost in all types of gas compression applications. The full line of API 11P high-speed reciprocating (HSR) compressors provide Economic, Flexible and Reliable solutions to meet your gas compression needs. Products range from 60 hp (45 kW) to 9,000 hp (6,714 kW), with a variety of piston rod load capacities and frame stroke combinations. HSR compressors are designed to be directly connected to reciprocating natural gas engines (720 to 1,800 rpm) and electric motors (750 to 1,800 rpm). A variety of compressor frame strokes allow the compressor to be perfectly matched to the optimum driver. BHGE has added couple-free high-speed, low vibration compressors, namely CFA, CFH and CFR, which assist customers cut capital and operating costs.
