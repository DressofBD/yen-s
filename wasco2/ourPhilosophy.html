TO ACCELERATE THE GLOBAL ENERGY TRANSITION WHILE ENHANCING VALUE FOR ALL OUR STAKEHOLDERS.
Our efforts to accelerate the global shift to renewable energy sources will deliver greater value to all with the following sustainability priorities:

  • Governance

    To strengthen relationships with stakeholders for a sustainable business development.

    Operational Efficiency

    To increase efficiency with continuous improvements in operations and supply chain.

    Environmental Stewardship

    To constantly measure and efficiently manage our environmental footprint in every aspect of our operations.

    To contribute to the markets we serve by aligning with national and global development goals.

  • Health and Safety

    To achieve TARGET ZERO (All Incidents and Accidents Are Preventable) by promoting health and safety of our stakeholders, employees, contractors and communities.

    Economic Responsibility

    To garner mutual beneficial economic value for our organisation and stakeholders.

    Product & Service Innovation

    To continually create new products, services and processes that will elevate customer experiences.

    People Empowerment

    To create effective, conducive and empowering workplace best practices so that our employees can be the best they can be.
